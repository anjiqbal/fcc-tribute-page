Wilson

The volleyball that helped a castaway survive years stranded on an island.

Six Facts about Wilson:

  • Wilson was reliable, quiet and a great listener.
  • Although he was also seriously affected by being stranded on a deserted island, Wilson found great comfort supporting his good friend.
  • Prior to becoming a castaway, Wilson was a professional volleyball player.
  • Wilson's appearance changed massively while he was stranded. He grew his hair out, lost weight and gained a face.
  • Wilson never developed a taste for seafood.
